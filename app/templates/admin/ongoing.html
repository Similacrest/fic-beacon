Import OPML

Export your subscriptions from your RSS reader (e.g. Preferences → Import/Export → Export OPML). Imported serials land in the Inbox on the Dashboard — assign each to a channel before it appears in drops. Note: AO3/FFN have no per-chapter RSS — those aren't supported here yet. Alternatively, use the Library page to add feeds inferred from your Calibre source URLs (RoyalRoad, SpaceBattles, SufficientVelocity).
